The API & QTrap Legacy Product Workshop covers parts of installation, maintenance, troubleshooting and repair. The course will cover the differences in the ion optics and tuning of Legacy instruments compared to Blueline. The FSE performs several exercises to develop the skills of servicing SCIEX products. The fundamentals of troubleshooting the LC/MS system using diagnostic tools will also be covered. Out of scope tasksIt is important to understand that the FSE is not fully certified to service all Sciex Mass Spectrometers. This training includes only Legacy instruments.  This training does not certify the FSE to work on Medical Device (MD) systems. This course will not include the installation and servicing of HPLC devices.
